How It Works

Character Sheet Formula Result ───────────────── ─────── ────── Defense Skill (B1) ── minus 350 ──▶ Skill from Gear ──× 0.04──▶ Crit Reduction % ╲ + = Total ╱ Resilience Rating (B2) ──────────÷ 39.4231──────────▶ Crit Reduction %
There is no single "resilience to defense" conversion. Both stats convert to crit reduction % through different rates, and the percentages simply add together. That's the key insight.

Crush Immunity (102.4% Combat Table)

Crushing blows deal 150% normal damage and can only be landed by mobs 3+ levels above you (i.e. level 73 raid bosses). Heroic dungeon bosses (level 72) cannot crush.

To push crushing blows off the single-roll combat table, your combined avoidance must reach 102.4%: Miss + Dodge + Parry + Block ≥ 102.4%.

Holy Shield adds 30% block chance while active, which is why paladins are the easiest tanks to make uncrushable.

Block Rating is by far the cheapest way to reach uncrushable (7.88 rating per 1% vs 18.92 for dodge or 23.65 for parry). Combined with Holy Shield's 30%, this is why paladins are the only tank class that can realistically reach 102.4% in early tiers.

Resilience does not help here at all — it only reduces crit chance, not the combat table.

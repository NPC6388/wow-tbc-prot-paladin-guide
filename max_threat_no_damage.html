Prot Paladin: Max Threat Rotation

When you're not taking damage — only offensive abilities work

What Goes Offline

When you aren't being hit, three major threat sources — and your entire mana engine — shut down:

Source Why It's Dead Threat Lost
Holy Shield OFFLINE Procs on block. No incoming attacks = no blocks = 0 procs. ~342 threat per block × 8 charges = massive loss
Retribution Aura OFFLINE Reflects damage when hit. No hits = no reflection. 49.4 threat per hit taken (96 TPS on AOE)
Blessing of Sanctuary OFFLINE Procs on block. Same as Holy Shield. 87.4 threat per block
Spiritual Attunement OFFLINE Returns mana when healed. Not taking damage = no heals = no mana return. Not a threat source, but kills your mana engine
Critical: Spiritual Attunement is how paladin tanks sustain mana in combat. With it offline, you are running on base regen + pots/runes only. Full rotation burns ~635 mana per 8s cycle. You will OOM in ~30–40 seconds going all-out.

What Still Works

Offensive Ability Priority (@ 300 Spell Power)

# Ability Cooldown Threat Notes
1 Judgement of Blood ACTIVE 8 sec (talented) ~834 Highest per-GCD. Recast SoR immediately after judging.
2 Consecration ACTIVE 8 sec ~1,165 (over 8s) Biggest total threat per cast. First to drop when mana-starved.
3 Avenger's Shield ACTIVE 30 sec ~1,093 High burst on CD. 6 sec daze.
4 Exorcism ACTIVE 15 sec ~1,412 Undead/Demon ONLY. If available, jumps to #2 priority.
5 Auto-attack + SoR procs ACTIVE weapon speed ~110 TPS passive Free. Runs automatically with Seal of Righteousness active.
6 Avenging Wrath ACTIVE 3 min +30% all damage Use on pull / opener. Causes Forbearance.

Seal Swap Rotation (Maximum Threat)

With the freed-up GCDs (no Holy Shield), seal swapping becomes free — you no longer have to choose between seal swap GCDs and Holy Shield. This yields ~193 TPS from seals alone, roughly 27% more than either seal used exclusively.

  2. 1.5s: Judgement of Blood (strongest Judgement)
  3. 3.0s: Seal of Righteousness (resume per-swing procs)
  4. 4.5s: Consecration
  5. 6.0s: Avenger's Shield / Exorcism / [free]

Judgement of Blood: (310 + 300×0.43) × 1.9 = ~834 threat

Avenger's Shield (amortized over 30s): ~1,093 / 30 × 8 = ~291 threat per cycle

Total per 8s cycle: ~3,639 threat (~455 TPS)

With Avenging Wrath (+30%): ~592 TPS for the first 20 seconds

The Mana Problem

Why Mana Is Critical Here

Normally, taking damage triggers healing which triggers Spiritual Attunement (10% of healing received as mana). This is your primary mana engine. When you're not taking damage, it's completely dead.

You also lose the Seal of Blood self-damage → heal → mana loop.

You're running on fumes: base mana regen + Super Mana Potions + Dark Runes only.

Full Burst Opener Sequence

  2. Avenging Wrath (+30% all damage for 20 sec)
  3. Avenger's Shield (ranged pull / opener burst)
  4. Seal of Blood (swap for judge)

Tip: If you're Horde, weave Arcane Torrent (no GCD) between abilities for free mana from Mana Tap charges (~171 mana per charge, up to 3). Every bit of mana counts when Spiritual Attunement is offline.
Tip: If the target is Undead or Demon, Exorcism (~1,412 threat at 300 SP) becomes your second-highest priority ability behind Judgement of Blood. Use it on every 15s cooldown — it's worth more per-GCD than Consecration on single target.

Gear Optimization: Stack Spell Power

Since ~85% of your threat scales with spell power when not taking damage, the natural conclusion is: swap out mitigation/avoidance gear for spell power gear. You're not getting hit — stamina, armor, dodge, and parry are doing nothing for you. Every gear slot spent on survival stats you don't need is threat you're leaving on the table.

What to Swap

Slot Swap From Swap To Why
Weapon Mallet of the Tides (+34 Stam, +16 Def, +14 Expertise, 0 SP) Gavel of Unearthed Secrets (+159 SP, +15 Spell Crit) or Continuum Blade (+121 SP, +8 Spell Hit) Your biggest single-slot SP gain. Tank weapon stats are wasted here.
Rings / Neck / Cloak Defense / Stamina / Avoidance pieces Spell power / Spell hit / Spell crit pieces Jewelry is the easiest swap — no armor class restriction. Caster rings and necks carry massive SP.
Trinkets Stamina / Armor / Avoidance trinkets Spell power trinkets (e.g., Icon of the Silver Crescent, Scryer's Bloodgem) Active-use SP trinkets also give burst threat on demand.
Gems Stamina / Defense gems Spell power gems (Runed Living Ruby, Glowing Nightseye) If running a dedicated off-tank threat set, gem entirely for SP + spell hit.
Enchants Defense / Stamina enchants Spell power enchants (Weapon: Major Spellpower, Chest: Exceptional Stats or Major Spirit for regen) Superior Wizard Oil on weapon is already standard for threat sets.

What NOT to Drop

Keep your safety net. Even as an off-tank not currently taking damage, maintain these minimums in case you suddenly need to pick up adds or the main tank dies:
  • 490 Defense — non-negotiable. One crit while emergency tanking = death. Defense also contributes to the 102.4% crush cap.
  • 102.4% combined avoidance (with Holy Shield active) — if you might need to tank a raid boss at any point during the fight, stay uncrushable. Holy Shield provides 30%, so you need 72.4% from gear (miss + dodge + parry + block).
  • Enough HP to survive a hit — if there's any chance of taking a boss swing, keep enough stamina to not get one-shot. A dead off-tank generates 0 TPS.
Build a dedicated spell power threat set. This is a third gear set alongside your boss mitigation and AOE sets. Hit the defense cap and crush cap minimums, then pour every remaining stat point into spell power, spell hit (until capped), and spell crit. This maximizes your threat contribution during phases where you're DPSing a boss without tanking it — exactly the scenario this page covers.

Should You Swap to a Physical DPS Weapon?

Since you're not blocking anyway, is there an argument for equipping a high-DPS melee weapon (or even a 2H) instead of your spell power tank weapon? No — and here's why.

Threat Source Breakdown (Not Taking Damage)

Threat Source Scales With % of Total TPS
Consecration Spell Power (1.0 coeff) ~45%
Judgement of Blood Spell Power (0.43 coeff) ~20%
Avenger's Shield Spell Power (0.091 coeff) ~8%
Exorcism (if applicable) Spell Power (0.429 coeff) ~9%
Seal of Righteousness procs Spell Power (0.2 coeff) + weapon speed ~13%
SoB procs / Auto-attacks Weapon Damage / Attack Power ~5–8%
Key insight: ~90% of your threat scales with spell power. Only auto-attacks and Seal of Blood's weapon damage component (~5–8% of total) scale with raw weapon DPS / attack power.

What You'd Gain

Benefit Impact
Bigger Seal of Blood procs 35% of weapon damage — a 200 DPS / 3.5 speed 2H = 700 dmg/swing → 245 Holy × 1.9 = 465 threat/swing
vs. tank 1H (100 DPS / 1.8 speed) = 180 dmg/swing → 63 Holy × 1.9 = 120 threat/swing
Harder white hits Higher auto-attack damage, but white damage generates 0 bonus threat (no Righteous Fury modifier)

What You'd Lose

Cost Impact
121–159 Spell Power from tank weapons Feeds every single ability in your rotation. At 1.35–1.65 TPS per SP (after the Consecration coefficient fix), that's ~165–265 TPS lost across Consecration, Judgement, Avenger's Shield, Exorcism, and SoR procs.
One-Handed Weapon Specialization (3/3) +10% damage with 1H weapons. Gone if you equip a 2H. This multiplies all 1H damage including seal procs.
Shield stats Shields like Aegis of the Vindicator carry +46 spell damage, stamina, and defense. Even without blocking, these stats contribute to threat and survivability if you suddenly need to pick up adds.
Spell Hit / Spell Crit on tank weapons Continuum Blade has +8 Spell Hit; Gavel has +15 Spell Crit. These improve consistency on Judgement, Consecration, and Avenger's Shield — your primary threat tools.

The Per-Point Comparison

Guide valuation:
1 Spell Power ≈ 1.2–1.5 TPS (scales Consecration, Judgement, Holy Shield, Avenger's Shield, SoR)
1 Strength (= 2 AP) ≈ 0.5–0.7 TPS (scales auto-attack, SoB weapon damage, block value)

Spell power is roughly 2× as valuable as attack power for paladin threat.

2H Weapon: SoB TPS Comparison

2H weapon (200 DPS, 3.5 speed, 0 SP):
SoB per swing: 700 × 0.35 × 1.9 = 465 threat
SoB TPS: 465 / 3.5 = ~133 TPS (from SoB only)

Tank 1H (100 DPS, 1.8 speed, +121 SP):
SoB per swing: 180 × 0.35 × 1.9 = 120 threat
SoB TPS: 120 / 1.8 = ~67 TPS (from SoB only)

SoB gain from 2H: +66 TPS
SP loss from dropping tank weapon: 121 SP × ~1.35 TPS/SP = −163 TPS
One-Handed Weapon Spec loss: −10% all 1H damage

Net: roughly −100 TPS or worse. The 2H loses.

The One Exception: OOM Auto-Attack Only

Edge case: If you are completely OOM and reduced to pure auto-attacking (SoR persists until judged), a high-DPS weapon would technically generate more threat from white hits + SoR procs than a 100 DPS caster mace. But this is a failure state, not a rotation choice. If you're OOM and not taking damage, something has gone very wrong with the encounter.

Verdict

Keep your spell power weapon. Paladin threat is a spell-damage class wearing plate. Even without taking damage, ~85% of your active rotation is spell-based. Losing 120+ SP from your weapon to gain bigger auto-attacks is a net TPS loss. The math doesn't close — not with a DPS 1H, and not with a 2H.

Case Study: Gruul Off-Tank (Hurtful Strike Soaker)

Gruul the Dragonkiller is the perfect encounter for a spell power off-tank set. The Hurtful Strike mechanic has unique properties that make defense cap and crush immunity completely unnecessary for the #2 threat position.

Gruul's Key Mechanics

Ability Effect Frequency
Growth +15% damage per stack, +10% size per stack. Stacks to 30. Every 30 sec
Hurtful Strike 12,350–13,650 physical damage to 2nd highest threat in melee range ~Every 8 sec
Ground Slam AoE knockback → Gronn Lord's Grasp (20% slow, stacks 5× → Stoned) ~Every 70 sec
Shatter Raid-wide damage based on proximity (~1,100 at 15+ yards, up to 8,500+ if stacked) Follows Ground Slam
Cave In ~3,000 physical damage every 3 sec in targeted area. Scales with Growth. Periodic (more frequent later)
Reverberation 4-second AoE silence (resistible) Periodic

Why Hurtful Strike Changes Everything

Key mechanic: Hurtful Strike cannot crit and cannot be a crushing blow. It is a flat physical hit mitigated only by armor, block, dodge, and parry. This has massive gearing implications.
Tanking Requirement Normal Boss Tanking Gruul Off-Tank (Hurtful Strike)
490 Defense (crit immunity) Non-negotiable. One crit = death. NOT NEEDED. Hurtful Strike cannot crit. You are not the MT — Gruul's normal melee swings target the MT only.
102.4% avoidance (crush immunity) Required. Crushing blows deal 150% damage. NOT NEEDED. Hurtful Strike cannot crush. Boss auto-attacks go to the MT.
High armor Important for physical bosses. YOUR PRIMARY DEFENSE. Hurtful Strike is physical. Armor is the only stat reducing every hit.
Stamina / HP Need to survive burst combos. CRITICAL. Must survive Hurtful Strike at high Growth stacks without getting one-shot.
Threat generation Hold aggro above all DPS. THE REAL CHALLENGE. Must stay #2 on threat — above every melee DPS — or they eat a Hurtful Strike and die.
Critical: If you drop below a melee DPS player on threat, they take the next Hurtful Strike. A 13,000+ physical hit on a rogue or fury warrior is almost certainly a one-shot. Your job is to hold #2 threat — the spell power set exists to make this possible. Pets are not valid Hurtful Strike targets — warlock/hunter pets cannot soak for you. If no second player is in melee range, Hurtful Strike hits the Main Tank instead.

The Mana Twist: Spiritual Attunement Is Partially Online

Unlike the pure "not taking damage" scenario covered above, the Gruul OT does take damage — one Hurtful Strike every ~8 seconds, which aligns almost perfectly with your 8-second rotation cycle. This nearly fully restores your mana engine:

Hurtful Strike hit (Growth 5, 14k armor): ~10,488 damage
Healer tops you off: ~10,488 effective healing
Spiritual Attunement (10%): ~1,049 mana returned per hit

Hurtful Strike frequency: ~every 8 sec = 1 hit per rotation cycle
Per 8s rotation cycle cost (seal swap): ~1,094 mana
SA return per 8s cycle: ~1,049 mana

Net mana deficit per 8s cycle: 1,094 − 1,049 = ~45 mana

vs. pure "no damage" deficit: ~1,094 mana per cycle
You are nearly mana-neutral. Spiritual Attunement covers ~96% of your rotation costs from Hurtful Strike healing alone. The remaining ~45 mana/cycle deficit is trivially covered by base MP5 regen, Blessing of Wisdom, or occasional Judgement of Wisdom procs. You can sustain the full seal swap rotation for the entire fight without degradation — a massive advantage over the pure no-damage scenario where you OOM in under a minute. As Growth stacks increase, Hurtful Strike hits harder, you receive more healing, and SA returns even more mana — your sustain actually improves as the fight goes on.

Additional mana sources on this fight:

  • Seal of Wisdom judged on Gruul — melee DPS proc 121 mana per hit. Consider judging Wisdom instead of Blood if mana is critical and DPS are providing enough threat cushion.
  • Blessing of Wisdom — ~41 MP5 passive if another paladin covers Sanctuary/Kings.
  • Arcane Torrent (Blood Elf) — free ~513 mana every 2 minutes from Mana Tap charges.
  • Shatter/Cave In damage — raid-wide damage triggers heals, which trigger more SA mana.

Recommended Gear Approach

Stat Priority Target
Spell Power #1 As high as possible. Every point = ~1.2–1.5 TPS. This is how you hold #2 threat.
Stamina #2 16,000+ HP buffed (17,000+ for safer margin past Growth 10).
Armor #3 Keep plate + shield equipped. ~14,000+ armor. Devotion Aura from another paladin adds ~1,292.
Spell Hit #4 Cap (16% / 202 rating) if possible. A resisted Judgement is a huge threat loss.
Defense / Avoidance Low Not needed. No crits, no crushes. Dodge/parry are a bonus if they come free on plate, but don't gem or enchant for them.
Block Rating / Block Value Low Holy Shield gives 30% block chance passively. Blocking a Hurtful Strike reduces it by your block value (~200–300), which is negligible on a 13,000+ hit.
This is your third gear set. You already have a boss mitigation set (490 def, crush immune) and an AOE threat set (SP + block). The Gruul OT set is: plate + shield for armor/HP, everything else spell power. Gem SP, enchant SP, use SP trinkets (Icon of the Silver Crescent, Scryer's Bloodgem). You are a caster in plate.

What About Dodge / Parry / Block?

These stats still work against Hurtful Strike — a dodge or parry avoids the hit entirely, and a block reduces it by your block value. But they are unreliable as a strategy:

  • Hurtful Strike hits once every ~8 seconds. You get one chance to dodge/parry/block per strike. With 15% combined avoidance (no defense stacking), you dodge/parry roughly 1 in 7 Hurtful Strikes. That's nice when it happens, but you can't gear around it.
  • Block value is negligible. Blocking removes ~200–300 damage from a 13,000+ hit. That's <2.5% mitigation. Armor does far more per item budget.
  • Every stat point in avoidance is a stat point not in spell power. And spell power is what keeps a melee DPS from taking the next Hurtful Strike and dying.

Gruul OT Rotation (Seal Swap)

Full Rotation — Sustain as Long as Mana Allows

  1. Pre-pull: Seal of Righteousness + Righteous Fury + Avenging Wrath
  2. 0.0s: Avenger's Shield (ranged pull assist / burst threat)
  3. 1.5s: Seal of Blood
  4. 3.0s: Judgement of Blood
  5. 4.5s: Seal of Righteousness
  6. 6.0s: Consecration
  7. 8.0s: Repeat from step 2

Degrade Consecration first when mana dips below 50%. Your Spiritual Attunement returns extend the full rotation significantly compared to pure no-damage scenarios.

Ground Slam recovery: After each Ground Slam knockback, immediately Sprint/move back into melee range. If you're out of melee range when Hurtful Strike fires, it targets the next person on threat — which is a DPS player who will die. Use Blessing of Freedom (if available from another paladin) to remove the Gronn Lord's Grasp slow and get back in faster.

Verdict: Gruul Off-Tank

YES — the spell power off-tank set works perfectly here. Hurtful Strike's inability to crit or crush makes defense cap and crush immunity dead stats. Plate armor + shield + stamina handle the raw physical damage, while spell power keeps you locked at #2 threat above every melee DPS. You even get partial Spiritual Attunement mana return from Hurtful Strike healing, extending your rotation sustainability well beyond the pure no-damage scenario. This encounter is the textbook case for the max-threat-not-tanking gear philosophy.

## 1. Consumables

### 1.1 Flask Selection

**Flask of Fortification** (Primary Tank Flask)
- Effect: +500 HP and +10 Defense Rating (2hr, persists through death)
- Materials: 7x Ancient Lichen, 3x Mana Thistle, 1x Fel Lotus, 1x Imbued Vial
- Best for: Boss tanking where survivability is paramount
- Cost: High (Ancient Lichen requires dungeon farming)

**Flask of Blinding Light** (Threat/DPS Flask)
- Effect: +80 Holy Spell Damage (2hr, persists through death)
- Materials: 7x Netherbloom, 3x Mana Thistle, 1x Fel Lotus, 1x Imbued Vial
- Best for: AOE tanking scenarios, Heroic dungeons, trash packs
- Use case: When threat is more important than survivability

**Comparison:**
- **Boss Tanking:** Flask of Fortification provides the defense rating for uncrittability and significant health pool
- **AOE/Trash Tanking:** Flask of Blinding Light maximizes Consecration threat and Holy Shield damage
- **Heroic 5-mans:** Flask of Blinding Light for superior threat generation

### 1.2 Elixir Combinations

Elixirs are more cost-effective than flasks and allow Battle + Guardian combinations.

**Battle Elixirs (Threat-focused):**
- **Adept's Elixir:** +24 Spell Damage and +24 Spell Crit (1hr)
  - Materials: 1x Dreamfoil, 1x Felweed, 1x Crystal Vial
  - Budget option for threat generation

**Guardian Elixirs (Survivability-focused):**
- **Elixir of Major Defense:** +550 Armor (1hr)
  - Materials: 3x Ancient Lichen, 1x Terocone, 1x Crystal Vial
  - Best armor option for physical damage mitigation

- **Elixir of Major Fortitude:** +250 HP and +10 HP5 (1hr)
  - Materials: 2x Ragveil, 1x Felweed, 1x Crystal Vial
  - Solid health buffer, budget-friendly

- **Elixir of Major Mageblood:** +16 MP5 (1hr)
  - Materials: 1x Ancient Lichen, 1x Netherbloom, 1x Crystal Vial
  - Critical for mana-intensive encounters

- **Earthen Elixir:** -20 All Damage Taken (1hr)
  - Materials: 1x Dreaming Glory, 2x Ragveil, 1x Crystal Vial
  - Flat damage reduction, extremely powerful

**Recommended Combinations:**
- **Boss Tanking:** Elixir of Major Defense + Earthen Elixir (armor + flat DR)
- **High Physical Damage:** Elixir of Major Defense + Elixir of Major Fortitude
- **Mana-Intensive Fights:** Elixir of Major Mageblood + Adept's Elixir
- **AOE Tanking:** Adept's Elixir + Elixir of Major Fortitude

### 1.3 Food Buffs

**Stamina Food (Primary for Boss Tanking):**
- **Spicy Crawdad:** +30 Stamina and +20 Spirit (30 min)
  - Source: Fished from Highland Mixed School pools in Terokkar (Blackwind Lake, Lake Ere'Noru)
  - Requires ~350+ fishing skill
  - Gold standard for tank food

- **Fisherman's Feast:** +30 Stamina and +20 Spirit (30 min)
  - Materials: 1x Huge Spotted Feltail, 1x Goldenbark Apple, 1x Soothing Spices
  - Alternative to Spicy Crawdad if you cannot fish

**Spell Power Food (Threat-focused):**
- **Blackened Basilisk:** +23 Spell Damage and +20 Spirit (30 min)
  - Source: Chunk o' Basilisk from Terokkar Forest basilisks
  - Best for AOE tanking and heroic dungeons
  - Significantly boosts Consecration and Holy Shield threat

- **Crunchy Serpent:** +23 Spell Damage and +20 Spirit (30 min)
  - Source: Serpent Flesh from Blade's Edge Mountains serpents
  - Identical buff to Blackened Basilisk

**Agility Food (Avoidance):**
- **Grilled Mudfish / Warp Burger:** +20 Agility and +20 Spirit (30 min)
  - Provides dodge and armor from agility
  - Niche use for avoidance-heavy strategies

**Recommendation by Content:**
- **Raid Boss Tanking:** Spicy Crawdad (stamina for burst damage)
- **Heroic Dungeons / AOE Tanking:** Blackened Basilisk (threat generation)
- **Avoidance Strategy:** Grilled Mudfish (dodge stacking)

### 1.4 Potions

**Ironshield Potion** (Critical Tank Cooldown)
- Effect: +2500 Armor for 2 minutes
- Materials: 2x Ancient Lichen, 3x Mote of Earth, 1x Crystal Vial
- Use: Pop before physical damage burst phases
- Does NOT share cooldown with combat potions initially
- Essential for encounters like Bloodboil, Brutallus

**Super Mana Potion** (Primary Mana Recovery)
- Effect: Restores 1800-3000 Mana instantly
- Materials: 2x Dreaming Glory, 1x Felweed, 1x Crystal Vial
- Use: Critical for mana management on long fights
- Share 2-minute cooldown with other combat potions

**Super Healing Potion**
- Effect: Restores 1500-2500 HP instantly
- Materials: 2x Netherbloom, 1x Felweed, 1x Crystal Vial
- Use: Emergency health recovery

**Heroic Potion** (Pre-pull Buff)
- Effect: +70 Strength and +700 HP for 15 seconds
- Materials: 2x Terocone, 1x Ancient Lichen, 1x Crystal Vial
- Use: Pre-pull before boss engagement for initial threat

**Dark Rune / Demonic Rune** (Secondary Mana CD)
- Effect: Restores 900-1500 mana; costs 600-1000 HP (2 min CD)
- Dark Rune: Drops from Scholomance necromancers (tradeable)
- Demonic Rune: Drops from Felwood/Azshara demons (BoP)
- Independent cooldown from potions - provides second mana recovery option
- Extremely valuable for Paladin tanks (mana = threat)

**Free Action Potion**
- Effect: Immune to Stun/Movement Impairing for 30 seconds
- Materials: 2x Blackmouth Oil, 1x Stranglekelp, 1x Leaded Vial
- Use: Boss mechanics with stuns/roots (Leotheras, Nightbane fear)

### 1.5 Weapon Buffs

**Superior Wizard Oil**
- Effect: +42 Spell Damage (1hr; 5 charges)
- Materials: 3x Arcane Dust, 1x Nightmare Vine, 1x Imbued Vial
- Best weapon buff for Paladin tanks (boosts all holy damage)
- Use for both weapons if dual-wielding

**Adamantite Sharpening Stone / Weightstone**
- Effect: +12 Weapon Damage and +14 Crit Rating (1hr)
- NOT recommended for Paladin tanks (physical damage focus)

### 1.6 Scrolls and Other Buffs

**Scrolls** (+20 to primary stats, 30 min)
- Scroll of Stamina V: +20 Stamina
- Scroll of Intellect V: +20 Intellect
- Scroll of Strength V: +20 Strength
- Use when missing class buffs in smaller groups

**Armor Kits:**
- Heavy Knothide Armor Kit: +10 Stamina (permanent to chest/legs/hands/feet/head/shoulders)
- Essential for tank gear - provides significant health pool
- Materials: 3x Heavy Knothide Leather per kit

**Flame Cap** (Special Consumable)
- Effect: +80 Fire Spell Damage for 1 min (3 min CD; independent of potion CD)
- Source: Herbalism nodes in Zangarmarsh (Spawning Glen)
- Niche use - does not benefit Holy damage

### 1.7 Consumable Strategy: AOE vs Boss Tanking

**AOE Tanking Setup (Heroics, Trash):**
- Flask: Flask of Blinding Light (+80 Holy Spell Damage)
- Food: Blackened Basilisk (+23 Spell Damage)
- Weapon: Superior Wizard Oil (+42 Spell Damage)
- Elixirs: Adept's Elixir + Elixir of Major Mageblood (threat + mana)
- Potion: Super Mana Potion (maintain mana for Consecration spam)
- Goal: Maximum threat per second on multiple targets

**Boss Tanking Setup (Raid):**
- Flask: Flask of Fortification (+500 HP, +10 Defense)
- Food: Spicy Crawdad (+30 Stamina)
- Weapon: Superior Wizard Oil (+42 Spell Damage)
- Elixirs: Elixir of Major Defense + Earthen Elixir (armor + flat DR)
- Armor Kit: Heavy Knothide Armor Kit on all applicable slots
- Potions: Ironshield Potion (physical burst phases), Super Mana Potion (mana), Dark/Demonic Rune (secondary mana)
- Goal: Maximum survivability and consistent threat

**Protection Potion Cauldrons:**
- Cauldron of Major Fire/Shadow/Nature/Frost/Arcane Protection
- Produces 25x protection potions for raid-wide resistance fights
- Essential for Hydross, Leotheras, Felmyst, Kalecgos

## 2. Enchantments

### 2.1 Head Enchants

**Glyph of the Defender** (BiS for Tank)
- Effect: +16 Defense Rating and +17 Dodge Rating
- Source: Keepers of Time (Revered)
- Vendor: Alurmi in Caverns of Time
- Cost: ~100g
- Notes: Requires Revered with Keepers of Time (farm Old Hillsbrad / Black Morass)

**Glyph of the Gladiator** (PvP Alternative)
- Effect: +18 Stamina and +20 Resilience Rating
- Source: Various PvP factions (Honor Hold/Thrallmar)
- Not optimal for PvE tanking

### 2.2 Shoulder Enchants

**Greater Inscription of Warding** (Aldor - BiS for Survival)
- Effect: +15 Dodge Rating and +10 Defense Rating
- Source: The Aldor (Exalted)
- Vendor: Inscriber Saadon in Shattrath (Aldor Rise)
- Cost: ~100g
- Notes: Best survivability option

**Greater Inscription of the Knight** (Aldor - Threat Option)
- Effect: +15 Defense Rating and +10 Parry Rating
- Source: The Aldor (Exalted)
- Notes: Alternative if parry is valued

**Greater Inscription of Vengeance** (Scryer - Threat Option)
- Effect: +15 Spell Crit Rating and +13 Spell Damage
- Source: The Scryers (Exalted)
- Vendor: Inscriber Veredis in Shattrath (Scryer's Tier)
- Notes: Threat-focused option for spell power build

**Greater Inscription of the Orb** (Scryer)
- Effect: +12 Spell Crit Rating and +15 Spell Damage
- Source: The Scryers (Exalted)
- Notes: Maximum threat, less survivability

**Recommendation:**
- **Boss Tanking:** Greater Inscription of Warding (Aldor)
- **Threat-Focused / AOE:** Greater Inscription of Vengeance (Scryer)
- Aldor vs Scryer is a major decision - see Section 4.6 for profession synergy

### 2.3 Chest Enchants

**Enchant Chest - Exceptional Health**
- Effect: +150 Health
- Materials: 8x Primal Life
- Best survivability option

**Enchant Chest - Major Resilience**
- Effect: +15 Resilience Rating
- Materials: 4x Greater Planar Essence, 2x Large Prismatic Shard
- PvP-focused, provides crit immunity contribution

**Enchant Chest - Exceptional Stats** (High-end option)
- Effect: +6 All Stats
- Materials: 4x Large Prismatic Shard, 2x Primal Air, 2x Primal Earth, 2x Primal Fire, 2x Primal Water
- Expensive, provides +6 Stamina/Intellect/Strength/Agility/Spirit
- Very versatile, excellent for min-maxing

**Recommendation:** Enchant Chest - Exceptional Health (cost-effective survivability)

### 2.4 Cloak Enchants

- Materials: 3x Greater Planar Essence, 1x Primal Earth

## 3. Gems

### 3.1 Meta Gems

**Eternal Earthstorm Diamond** (BiS for Tanks)
- Effect: +12 Defense Rating and +5% Shield Block Value
- Activation Requirement: At least 2 more Blue gems than Red gems
- Socket: Meta
- Source: Jewelcrafting (375) - Recipe from Shattrath Violet Eye quartermaster
- Essential for reaching defense cap and boosting Holy Shield
- Must maintain blue > red + 2 gem ratio

**Bracing Earthstorm Diamond** (Alternative)
- Effect: +12 Defense Rating and 2% reduced threat
- Activation: At least 2 more Blue gems than Red gems
- Use case: Off-tank scenarios where threat reduction is desired
- Not recommended for main tank

**Powerful Earthstorm Diamond** (DPS Meta)
- Effect: +18 Stamina and 2% reduced threat
- Not optimal for threat-focused tank builds

**Gem Strategy for Meta Activation:**
- Requires 2 more Blue gems than Red gems
- Example: 6 Blue, 4 Red, X Yellow = Valid
- Plan gem distribution carefully to maintain activation

### 3.2 Stamina Gems (Blue)

**Solid Star of Elune** (Rare Blue - Pure Stamina)
- Effect: +12 Stamina
- Socket: Blue
- Best pure health gem
- Use in blue sockets for socket bonus

**Enduring Talasite** (Uncommon Green - Stam/Defense)
- Effect: +6 Stamina and +5 Defense Rating
- Socket: Blue or Yellow
- Excellent for defense-capped sets
- Hybrid gem for yellow sockets

**Shifting Nightseye** (Uncommon Purple - Stam/Agi)
- Effect: +6 Stamina and +5 Agility
- Socket: Blue or Red
- Provides avoidance with stamina
- Good for red sockets when maintaining meta requirement

### 3.3 Defense Gems (Blue/Yellow)

**Solid Star of Elune** (Primary Defense Gem via Stamina)
- See above - stamina provides effective HP

**Enduring Talasite** (Stam/Defense Hybrid)
- +6 Stamina and +5 Defense Rating
- Most efficient defense rating gem for reaching 490 def cap

**Pristine Talasite** (Uncommon Green - Defense/Spell Damage)
- Effect: +5 Defense Rating and +6 Spell Damage
- Socket: Blue or Yellow
- Threat-focused hybrid

**Defender's Nightseye** (Uncommon Purple - Defense/Parry)
- Effect: +5 Defense Rating and +5 Parry Rating
- Socket: Blue or Red

### 3.4 Spell Power Gems (Red/Yellow)

**Glowing Nightseye** (Uncommon Purple - Spell Damage/Stamina)
- Effect: +6 Spell Damage and +6 Stamina
- Socket: Blue or Red
- Best threat gem with survivability
- Use in red sockets for threat builds

**Potent Flame Spessarite** (Uncommon Orange - Spell Damage/Crit)
- Effect: +6 Spell Damage and +5 Spell Crit Rating

**Veiled Pyrestone** (Uncommon Orange - Spell Damage/Resilience)

**Reckless Pyrestone** (Uncommon Orange - Spell Damage/Haste)

### 3.5 Unique Gems (Jewelcrafting Only)

**Jewelcrafting Exclusive Gems (Prismatic):**
Jewelcrafters can use 3x Jewelcrafter-only gems that are more powerful than standard gems:

**Don Julio's Heart** (JC Only - Stamina)
- Effect: +18 Stamina
- Socket: Any (Prismatic)
- 50% better than Solid Star of Elune
- Limit: 3x Jewelcrafter-only gems total

**Reckless Pyrestone** (JC Only - Enhanced)
- Effect: +9 Spell Damage and +8 Haste Rating
- Socket: Any (Prismatic)
- Superior threat gem

**Stone of Blades** (JC Only - Crit/Hit)
- Effect: +8 Crit Rating and +8 Hit Rating
- Socket: Any (Prismatic)
- Excellent for threat-focused set

**Recommendation for JC Paladins:**
- Use 3x Don Julio's Heart for maximum survivability (+54 stamina vs +36)
- Or mix: 2x Don Julio's Heart + 1x JC spell damage gem for hybrid

### 3.6 Gem Strategy: AOE Set vs Boss Set

**Boss Tanking Gem Priority:**
1. Meta: Eternal Earthstorm Diamond
2. Blue Sockets: Solid Star of Elune (+12 Stamina)
3. Red Sockets: Glowing Nightseye (+6 Spell Damage / +6 Stamina) or Shifting Nightseye (+6 Stam / +5 Agi)
4. Yellow Sockets: Enduring Talasite (+6 Stam / +5 Defense) until defense capped
5. Socket Bonuses: Prioritize +stamina bonuses; ignore small bonuses if gem quality suffers
6. Maintain meta activation: 2 more blue than red gems
7. Goal: Reach 490 defense, maximize stamina, fill gaps with spell damage/avoidance

**AOE Tanking / Threat Set Gem Priority:**
1. Meta: Eternal Earthstorm Diamond
2. Blue Sockets: Solid Star of Elune (+12 Stamina) or Rigid Star of Elune (+12 Hit)
3. Red Sockets: Glowing Nightseye (+6 Spell Damage / +6 Stamina)
4. Yellow Sockets: Potent Flame Spessarite (+6 Spell Damage / +5 Crit)
5. Socket Bonuses: Prioritize +spell damage bonuses
6. Maintain meta activation: 2 more blue than red gems
7. Goal: Reach 9% spell hit, maximize spell damage, maintain 490 defense

**Defense Cap Calculation:**
- Base defense: ~365 at level 70
- Need 490 defense for uncrittable (140 defense rating = +17.5 defense)
- ~89 defense rating from gear + gems + enchants
- Prioritize defense rating until capped, then shift to stamina/avoidance

**Hit Cap for Threat:**
- 9% spell hit = 76 spell hit rating
- Critical for Seal of Righteousness/Vengeance and Consecration to land
- Reduces miss chance on judgements

### 3.7 Example Gemming Strategy

**Pre-Defense Cap (Still need defense rating):**
- Meta: Eternal Earthstorm Diamond
- Blue sockets: Enduring Talasite (+6 Stam / +5 Def) until capped
- Red sockets: Defender's Nightseye (+5 Def / +5 Parry) for meta req
- Yellow sockets: Enduring Talasite (+6 Stam / +5 Def)
- Fill gaps with defense rating gems until 490 defense

**Post-Defense Cap (Survivability Focus):**
- Meta: Eternal Earthstorm Diamond
- Blue sockets: Solid Star of Elune (+12 Stamina)
- Red sockets: Shifting Nightseye (+6 Stam / +5 Agi)
- Yellow sockets: Enduring Talasite (+6 Stam / +5 Def)
- Maximize stamina, use socket bonuses if +4 stamina or better

**Post-Defense Cap (Threat Focus):**
- Meta: Eternal Earthstorm Diamond
- Blue sockets: Solid Star of Elune (+12 Stamina) or Rigid Star of Elune (+12 Hit)
- Red sockets: Glowing Nightseye (+6 Spell Damage / +6 Stamina)
- Yellow sockets: Potent Flame Spessarite (+6 Spell Damage / +5 Crit)
- Reach 9% spell hit, then stack spell damage

## 4. Professions

### 4.1 Engineering (Tier 1 - Highest Utility)

**Benefits:**
- **Tankatronic Goggles:** Epic helm with excellent stats (Stam/Int/Spell Damage/Defense)
  - Requires Engineering (350) to equip
  - Pre-raid BiS, competitive through T4
  - Sockets: 1x Meta, 1x Yellow
  - Stats: ~30 Stamina, ~25 Intellect, ~30 Spell Damage, ~25 Defense Rating (varies by version)

- **Goblin Rocket Launcher:** Ranged pull/AOE threat tool
  - 3-second cast, ranged pull ability
  - ~450-650 fire damage
  - Shares 1-minute CD with other engineering items
  - Essential for positioning and ranged threat

- **Goblin Sapper Charge:** AOE threat burst
  - 450-750 fire damage in 10-yard radius
  - Excellent for AOE threat generation on trash
  - Can be used while moving

- **Super Sapper Charge:** Enhanced version
  - 675-1125 fire damage in 10-yard radius
  - More expensive but massive threat

- **Destruction Holo-gogs:** AOE fear
  - Fears up to 5 enemies for 3 seconds
  - Emergency AOE control tool

- **Nigh-Invulnerability Belt:** Emergency cheat death
  - 3-second cast absorb shield
  - Potential save mechanic

**Specialization Choice:**
- **Goblin Engineering:** Access to Goblin Sapper Charge, Goblin Rocket Launcher
- **Gnomish Engineering:** Access to Gnomish Battle Chicken (threat pet), Mind Control Cap
- **Recommendation:** Goblin for tank utility

### 4.2 Jewelcrafting (Tier 1 - Stat Advantage)

**Benefits:**
- **Exclusive Gems:** 3x Jewelcrafter-only gems (Prismatic sockets)
  - Don Julio's Heart: +18 Stamina (vs +12 normal) = +6 stamina per gem
  - Total advantage: +18 stamina (+54 vs +36) if using 3x stamina gems
  - Alternative: Mix threat/survival gems for hybrid optimization

- **Figurine Trinkets:** Various stat trinkets
  - Figurine - Empyrean Tortoise: +12 Defense Rating and on-use 1750 HP shield (2-min CD)
  - Figurine - Crimson Serpent: +12 Resilience and on-use +320 Spell Damage for 20 sec (2-min CD)
  - Not BiS but solid alternatives

- **Gem Cutting:** Self-sufficiency for gem swaps
  - No need to pay for gem cuts
  - Economic benefit for frequent regearing

**Recommendation:** Excellent for stat optimization and long-term gold savings

### 4.3 Enchanting (Tier 1 - Ring Enchants)

**Benefits:**
- **Ring Enchants:** Exclusive to Enchanters
  - Enchant Ring - Spellpower: +12 Spell Damage per ring (24 total)
  - Enchant Ring - Stats: +4 All Stats per ring (8 total)
  - Significant threat advantage: +24 spell damage = ~48 DPS to threat

- **Disenchanting:** Self-sufficiency for enchanting materials
  - Access to Arcane Dust, Greater Planar Essence, Large Prismatic Shard
  - Economic benefit: farm dungeons, DE greens/blues

- **No crafted gear requirements:** All benefit with no gear restrictions

**Recommendation:** Top-tier profession for pure stat gain

### 4.4 Blacksmithing (Tier 2 - Crafted Gear & Sockets)

**Benefits:**
- **Extra Gem Sockets:**
  - Socket Bracers: +1 socket to bracers
  - Socket Gloves: +1 socket to gloves
  - Total advantage: 2 additional gem sockets = ~+24 stamina or +12 spell damage + stats

- **Crafted Tanking Gear:**
  - Flamebane Bracers: High-stamina bracers with defense
  - Felsteel Helm: Pre-raid tanking helm option
  - Enchanted Adamantite Breastplate: Solid tanking chest
  - Khorium Champion: Epic shield (not optimal for Paladins due to no spell damage)

- **Weapon Chains:** (Not useful for Paladins - immune to disarm via class mechanics)

**Calculation:**
- 2 sockets = +24 stamina or +12 spell damage/+12 stam hybrid
- Competitive with Jewelcrafting (+18 stam) but more flexible

**Recommendation:** Solid choice for socket flexibility and crafted gear

### 4.5 Alchemy (Tier 2 - Consumable Efficiency)

**Benefits:**
- **Mixology (Potion Master):** Extended duration and bonus effects on flasks/elixirs
  - 2x flask duration (4 hours vs 2 hours)
  - Significant gold savings over progression

- **Transmutation Master:** Primal transmutation procs
  - Extra Primal Fires/Waters/Earths for raid consumables

- **Cauldron Crafting:** Provide resistance potions for raid
  - Economic benefit: craft cauldrons for guild

**Drawbacks:**
- No direct stat benefit
- Requires constant farming/gold investment
- Economic benefit only

**Recommendation:** Best paired with Herbalism for self-sufficiency; strong for hardcore raiders

### 4.6 Leatherworking (Tier 2 - Drums & Leg Enchants)

**Benefits:**
- **Drums of Battle:** +80 Haste Rating to party for 30 sec (2 min CD)
  - Requires Leatherworking (350) to use
  - Raid-wide DPS/HPS increase via rotation
  - Significant contribution to raid performance

- **Exclusive Leg Enchants:** Access before non-LW
  - (Note: Leg armors can be applied by anyone in TBC 2.3+)

- **Crafted Leather Tank Gear:** Limited for Paladins (plate focus)

**Recommendation:** Strong for raid utility, drums are valuable

### 4.7 Tailoring (Not Recommended)

**Benefits:**
- Spellcloth set bonuses: Not relevant for plate tanks
- Netherweave Net: Minor CC tool
- Flying carpet: Cosmetic

**Recommendation:** No meaningful benefit for Paladin tanks

### 4.8 Mining / Herbalism / Skinning (Gathering Professions)

**Benefits:**
- **Gold Generation:** High income for consumables
- **Ore/Herb Farming:** Self-sufficiency for Engineering/Alchemy
- **No stat benefits** (TBC has no gathering profession bonuses)

**Recommendation:**
- **Mining:** Pair with Engineering or Blacksmithing
- **Herbalism:** Pair with Alchemy
- **Skinning:** Pair with Leatherworking
- Generally drop gathering after accumulating gold/materials

### 4.9 Best Profession Combinations

**Min-Max Tanking (Stat Priority):**
1. **Engineering + Jewelcrafting**
   - Engineering: Tankatronic Goggles, Goblin Rocket Launcher, Sapper Charges
   - Jewelcrafting: +18 stamina from 3x prismatic gems
   - Total: +18 stamina, helm, utility tools

2. **Engineering + Enchanting**
   - Engineering: Utility tools + goggles
   - Enchanting: +24 spell damage on rings
   - Total: +24 threat, utility tools

3. **Enchanting + Jewelcrafting**
   - Enchanting: +24 spell damage on rings
   - Jewelcrafting: +18 stamina from 3x gems
   - Total: +18 stamina, +24 threat
   - No utility tools (lose rocket launcher, sappers)

**Economic / Raid Utility:**
4. **Engineering + Alchemy** (or Herbalism)
   - Engineering: Full utility
   - Alchemy: 4-hour flasks, consumable savings
   - Best for hardcore progression (consumable cost reduction)

5. **Leatherworking + Skinning**
   - Drums of Battle for raid DPS
   - Gold generation from leather

**Recommendation:**
- **Best Overall:** Engineering + Jewelcrafting (utility + stamina)
- **Best Threat:** Engineering + Enchanting (utility + spell damage)
- **Best Economic:** Engineering + Herbalism/Mining → Alchemy later

### 4.10 Faction Choice: Aldor vs Scryer

**The Aldor (Survivability):**
- Greater Inscription of Warding: +15 Dodge Rating and +10 Defense Rating (Exalted shoulder enchant)
- Vindicator's Brand (Honored): Spell damage 1H mace with crit
- Anchorite's Boon (Revered): Defense trinket
- Recommendation: Best for boss tanking, survivability focus

**The Scryers (Threat):**
- Greater Inscription of Vengeance: +15 Spell Crit Rating and +13 Spell Damage (Exalted shoulder enchant)
- Seer's Signet (Revered): Spell damage ring
- Recommendation: Best for threat-focused builds, AOE tanking

**Choice Impact:**
- **Boss Tanking:** Aldor (dodge + defense)
- **AOE/Heroic Tanking:** Scryer (spell damage + crit)
- Can switch factions later but very expensive (grind opposite rep, lose current progress)

## 5. Macros

### 5.1 Righteous Defense Taunt Macro

Righteous Defense requires targeting a friendly player who has aggro, making it awkward. This macro allows mouseover friendly targeting or direct casting.

```
#showtooltip Righteous Defense
/cast [target=mouseover,help,nodead] Righteous Defense; Righteous Defense
```

**Usage:**
- Mouseover a raid frame to taunt enemies off that player
- If no mouseover, casts normally on current friendly target
- Essential for reactive taunting

**Enhanced Version with Focus Target:**
```
#showtooltip Righteous Defense
/cast [target=mouseover,help,nodead] Righteous Defense; [target=focus,help,nodead] Righteous Defense; Righteous Defense
```

**Usage:**
- Mouseover takes priority
- Falls back to focus target (set focus on main tank for off-tank)
- Falls back to current target

### 5.2 Divine Shield + Cancel Macro (Emergency)

Divine Shield makes you immune but drops all threat. This macro allows instant cancel to resume tanking.

```
#showtooltip Divine Shield
/cast Divine Shield
/cancelaura Divine Shield
```

**Usage:**
- Tap once: Apply Divine Shield (full immunity)
- Tap twice: Cancel Divine Shield (resume threat)
- Use to survive one-shot mechanics, then immediately drop to continue tanking

**Alternative - Cancel Only:**
```
/cancelaura Divine Shield
```

**Usage:**
- Keybind separately to cancel Divine Shield after using it normally
- More controlled than the instant macro

### 5.3 Seal Casting Macro

Seals must be active to Judge. This macro streamlines seal activation:

```
#showtooltip Seal of Vengeance
/cast Seal of Vengeance
```

**Multi-Seal Macro (Modifier-based):**
```
#showtooltip
/cast [mod:shift] Seal of Wisdom; [mod:ctrl] Seal of Light; Seal of Vengeance
```

**Usage:**
- Default: Cast Seal of Vengeance (threat)
- Shift-modifier: Cast Seal of Wisdom (mana regen)
- Ctrl-modifier: Cast Seal of Light (healing)

### 5.4 Judgement Macro

Judgement consumes the active seal. This macro ensures quick judgement casting:

```
#showtooltip Judgement of Vengeance
/cast Judgement of Vengeance
```

**Seal + Judge Macro (NOT RECOMMENDED - GCD issues):**
```
/castsequence reset=8 Seal of Vengeance, Judgement of Vengeance
```

**Note:** Castsequence macros can desync if interrupted. Better to separate Seal and Judge keybinds.

### 5.5 Aura Switching Macros

Paladins have multiple auras for different scenarios. Quick switching is important.

```
#showtooltip
/cast [mod:shift] Devotion Aura; [mod:ctrl] Retribution Aura; Concentration Aura
```

**Usage:**
- Default: Concentration Aura (interrupt immunity)
- Shift: Devotion Aura (armor buff for group)
- Ctrl: Retribution Aura (damage reflection for threat)

**Alternative - Separate Keybinds:**
Bind each aura to a separate key for faster access during combat.

### 5.6 Gear Swap Macros (AOE Set vs Boss Set)

Equipment Manager addons (Outfitter, ItemRack) are better, but macros work for simple swaps:

**Simple 2-Piece Swap (Example: Shield + Weapon):**
```
/equipslot 16 [Spell Damage Weapon Name]
/equipslot 17 [Spell Damage Shield Name]
```

**Boss Tank Set Swap:**
```
/equipslot 16 [Stamina Weapon Name]
/equipslot 17 [Stamina Shield Name]
```

**Note:** Character names in brackets must match exactly. Use addons for complex gear swaps.

### 5.7 Engineering Item Macros

**Goblin Rocket Launcher Macro:**
```
#showtooltip Goblin Rocket Launcher
/use Goblin Rocket Launcher
```

**Sapper Charge Macro:**
```
#showtooltip Goblin Sapper Charge
/use Goblin Sapper Charge
```

**Combined Engineering Macro (Modifier-based):**
```
#showtooltip
/use [mod:shift] Goblin Sapper Charge; Goblin Rocket Launcher
```

**Usage:**
- Default: Goblin Rocket Launcher (ranged pull)
- Shift: Goblin Sapper Charge (AOE threat)

### 5.8 Consumable Popping Macro

**Multi-Consumable Pre-Pull:**
```
/use Super Mana Potion
/use Ironshield Potion
/use Dark Rune
```

**Note:** Only one consumable per GCD, but macros queue them for rapid use.

### 5.9 Trinket Macro

**On-Use Trinket Activation:**
```
#showtooltip [Trinket Name]
/use 13
```

**Usage:**
- Slot 13 = top trinket slot
- Slot 14 = bottom trinket slot

**Dual Trinket Macro:**
```
/use 13
/use 14
```

**Usage:** Activates both trinkets simultaneously (if off cooldown).

### 5.10 Focus Target Macro

**Set Focus on Off-Tank or Boss:**
```
/focus [target=target]
```

**Cast on Focus:**
```
/cast [target=focus] Cleanse
```

**Usage:** Quickly Cleanse the off-tank or dispel boss target without retargeting.

## 6. Addons

### 6.1 PallyPower (Blessing Management)

**Function:**
- Automated blessing assignments for raid
- Shows which Paladin is buffing which group/player
- One-click buffing for entire raid
- Tracks blessing durations and missing buffs

**Why Essential:**
- TBC raids have 25 players across 5 groups
- Paladins must coordinate Greater Blessings (30-min buffs)
- Prevents duplicate blessings and missed buffs
- Saves reagents (Symbol of Kings cost)

**Setup:**
- Assign Paladins to groups in raid
- PallyPower coordinates with other Paladins using the addon
- Buff entire raid with one click per blessing type

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.2 ThreatPlates / Omen (Threat Monitoring)

**ThreatPlates (Nameplate Threat):**
- Colors nameplates based on threat level
- Red = losing threat, Yellow = close to cap, Green = solid aggro
- Shows threat percentage on nameplates
- Essential for multitarget tanking

**Omen Threat Meter:**
- Raid-wide threat tracking
- Shows your threat vs DPS threat in real-time
- Alerts when DPS is close to pulling aggro
- Critical for threat management

**Why Essential:**
- Paladin AOE tanking generates threat on 5+ targets simultaneously
- Must monitor threat on each target to prevent adds breaking loose
- DPS must throttle based on Omen data

**Setup:**
- ThreatPlates: Enable threat coloring, set proximity distance
- Omen: Position threat meter on screen, enable audio warnings

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.3 PallyBuff / PallyRadar (Similar to PallyPower)

**Function:**
- Alternative to PallyPower for blessing management
- Tracks buff durations, alerts for rebuffing
- Less sophisticated than PallyPower but simpler

**Recommendation:** Use PallyPower instead (more raid coordination features)

### 6.4 WeakAuras (Custom Alerts)

**Function:**
- Highly customizable visual/audio alerts for ability cooldowns, procs, debuffs
- Track Holy Shield uptime, Avenger's Shield CD, Seal of Vengeance stacks
- Boss mechanic warnings (taunt rotations, debuff stacks)

**Essential WeakAuras for Paladin Tanks:**

1. **Holy Shield Uptime Tracker:**
   - Alert when Holy Shield drops (critical survivability cooldown)
   - Shows remaining duration and charges

2. **Avenger's Shield Cooldown:**
   - Large icon when Avenger's Shield is off CD
   - Essential for threat burst windows

3. **Seal of Vengeance Stack Counter:**
   - Shows SoV stacks on target (up to 5)
   - Maximizes threat when stacks are capped

4. **Consecration Timer:**
   - Tracks Consecration duration (8 seconds)
   - Alerts when Consecration expires (massive threat loss)

5. **Ardent Defender Proc Alert:**
   - Large visual when Ardent Defender procs (30% HP threshold)
   - Alerts healers you took massive damage

6. **Judgement Cooldown:**
   - Tracks 8-second Judgement CD
   - Optimal threat rotation timing

7. **Divine Protection / Divine Shield Ready:**
   - Tracks emergency cooldowns
   - Audio alert for available "oh crap" buttons

**Setup:**
- Import WeakAura strings from Paladin community (wago.io)
- Customize positioning, size, colors
- Test in combat to ensure visibility

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface, wago.io for imports

### 6.5 Deadly Boss Mods (DBM) / BigWigs

**Function:**
- Boss ability timers and warnings
- Audio alerts for tank swaps, debuff applications, incoming damage
- Critical for progression raiding

**DBM vs BigWigs:**
- DBM: More hand-holding, verbose warnings, popular
- BigWigs: More customizable, less spammy, preferred by hardcore raiders
- Both fully functional - personal preference

**Essential Features for Tanks:**
- Taunt rotation timers (Gruul, Magtheridon, Archimonde)
- Debuff stack warnings (Brutallus, Mother Shahraz)
- Incoming burst damage alerts (Bloodboil, Teron Gorefiend)

**Setup:**
- Enable tank-specific warnings
- Customize audio alerts for taunt mechanics
- Position timers prominently on screen

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.6 Grid / Healbot (Raid Frames)

**Function:**
- Compact raid frames for easy raid health monitoring
- Allows quick Lay on Hands / Cleanse targeting
- Shows debuffs, incoming heals, aggro status

**Grid:**
- Lightweight, highly customizable
- Requires additional plugins for full functionality
- Preferred by experienced raiders

**Healbot:**
- More automated, click-to-heal functionality
- Larger out-of-the-box feature set
- Easier for new players

**Usage for Tanks:**
- Monitor raid health for emergency Lay on Hands
- Cleanse debuffs on raid members (Cleanse removes Magic, Disease, Poison)
- Righteous Defense mouseover targeting

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.7 Outfitter / ItemRack (Gear Management)

**Function:**
- Quick gear swapping between AOE and Boss sets
- Automatic gear swaps based on zones, stances, etc.
- Saves equipment sets for one-click switching

**Outfitter:**
- More feature-rich, automatic swaps
- Can swap based on zone entry, combat state

**ItemRack:**
- Simpler, button-based gear swapping
- Less automatic, more manual control

**Usage for Paladin Tanks:**
- Create "AOE Threat Set" (spell damage focus)
- Create "Boss Survival Set" (stamina/defense focus)
- Swap between sets during raid (trash vs boss)

**Setup:**
- Create equipment sets in addon
- Bind gear swap to hotkey or macro
- Configure automatic swaps if desired

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.8 OmniCC (Cooldown Timers)

**Function:**
- Shows numerical cooldown timers on action bar icons
- Easier to track CDs than default radial sweep

**Usage:**
- Track Avenger's Shield (30s), Judgement (8s), Holy Shield (8s)
- Know exact CD time remaining

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.9 Bartender / Dominos (Action Bar Addon)

**Function:**
- Customizable action bars (size, position, number of bars)
- Cleaner UI than default Blizzard bars

**Usage:**
- Position abilities for optimal combat flow
- Scale bars for easier visibility

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

### 6.10 Recount / Details! (Damage/Threat Meter)

**Function:**
- Tracks damage, healing, threat, deaths
- Post-raid analysis for threat performance

**Usage for Tanks:**
- Verify threat generation vs DPS
- Analyze Holy Shield / Consecration uptime
- Review avoidance stats (dodge/parry/block)

**Download:** Curse, WoWInterface

## 7. Race Selection

### 7.1 Alliance Races (Human, Dwarf, Draenei)

**Human (Strong Overall)**
- **The Human Spirit:** +3% Spirit
  - Minor benefit: Increases mana regen slightly
  - Not impactful for tanks

- **Sword Specialization / Mace Specialization:** +5 Weapon Skill
  - MAJOR benefit: +5 Weapon Skill = ~1.2% reduced chance to be parried/dodged
  - Allows use of Sword/Mace weapons with better threat
  - Examples: Continuum Blade, Fang of the Leviathan

- **Perception:** Stealth detection
  - Irrelevant for PvE tanking

- **Diplomacy:** +10% reputation gain
  - Quality of life: Faster faction grinding (Aldor/Scryer, Keepers of Time)

**Verdict:** Strong choice for weapon flexibility and rep grinding

---

**Dwarf (Defensive Utility)**
- **Stoneform:** Removes all poison, disease, bleed effects and grants +10% armor for 8 seconds (3-min CD)
  - MAJOR benefit: Emergency armor cooldown (stacks with Ironshield Potion)
  - Removes bleeds (Lacerates on Brutallus, etc.)
  - +10% armor = ~1000-1500 additional armor on a geared tank

- **Frost Resistance:** +10 Frost Resistance
  - Minor benefit: Small help on frost-heavy bosses (Hydross ice phase)

- **Gun Specialization:** +5 Gun Skill
  - Irrelevant for Paladins (no ranged weapon slot)

**Verdict:** Best defensive racial for tanks, Stoneform is powerful

---

**Draenei (Threat Support)**
- **Heroic Presence:** +1% spell hit to party
  - MAJOR benefit: Entire party gains 1% spell hit (12.6 hit rating)
  - Reduces personal hit cap requirement from 76 to 63.4 spell hit rating
  - Massive benefit for caster DPS in party

- **Gift of the Naaru:** HoT heal (scales with Spell Power / Attack Power)
  - Minor benefit: Small self-heal (~1500-2000 HP over 15 sec)
  - Scales with spell damage on Paladin tanks

- **Shadow Resistance:** +10 Shadow Resistance
  - Minor benefit: Small help on shadow-heavy bosses (Illidan, Mother Shahraz)

**Verdict:** Best for threat optimization and supporting caster DPS

---

### 7.2 Horde Race (Blood Elf - Only Option)

**Blood Elf (Strong Utility)**
- **Arcane Torrent:** Silences all enemies within 8 yards and restores 2% base mana (2-min CD)
  - MAJOR benefit: AOE silence for caster adds (Hyjal, trash packs)
  - Mana restoration: ~1000 mana every 2 minutes
  - Excellent for AOE tanking and mana management

- **Magic Resistance:** +5 Arcane, Fire, Frost, Nature, Shadow Resistance
  - Minor benefit: Small boost to all resistances (5 resistance = ~0.2% reduced chance to be hit by spells)

**Verdict:** Excellent overall choice, Arcane Torrent is extremely powerful for AOE tanking

---

### 7.3 Racial Comparison for Tanking

**Survivability Ranking:**
1. **Dwarf:** Stoneform (+10% armor on demand, bleed removal)
2. **Blood Elf:** Magic resistance (all schools)
3. **Human / Draenei:** Minimal defensive benefits

**Threat Ranking:**
1. **Draenei:** Heroic Presence (+1% spell hit to party, personal hit cap reduction)
2. **Human:** Weapon Skill (+5 sword/mace, better threat scaling)
3. **Blood Elf:** Arcane Torrent (AOE silence, mana regen)
4. **Dwarf:** No threat benefits

**Utility Ranking:**
1. **Blood Elf:** Arcane Torrent (AOE silence, mana regen)
2. **Dwarf:** Stoneform (debuff removal, armor CD)
3. **Human:** Diplomacy (rep grind speed)
4. **Draenei:** Heroic Presence (party-wide benefit)

**Overall Ranking for Paladin Tanks:**
1. **Dwarf (Alliance):** Best defensive cooldown, bleed removal essential on several bosses
2. **Draenei (Alliance):** Best threat support, benefits entire raid
3. **Human (Alliance):** Solid all-around, weapon flexibility
4. **Blood Elf (Horde):** Excellent utility, mandatory for Horde (only option)

### 7.4 Faction Choice Considerations

**Alliance:**
- Access to three Paladin races (Human, Dwarf, Draenei)
- Seal of Vengeance (DoT-based threat seal)
- Larger Paladin population (historically)

**Horde:**
- Only Blood Elf can be Paladin (TBC addition)
- Seal of Blood (high burst threat but self-damage)
- Arcane Torrent is extremely strong for AOE tanking

**Seal Differences:**
- **Seal of Vengeance (Alliance):** 5-stack DoT, ramps up threat, better for long boss fights
- **Seal of Blood (Horde):** Instant damage proc, higher burst threat, self-damage (not recommended for tanking)
- Alliance generally has better tanking seal

**Recommendation:**
- **Alliance:** Dwarf or Draenei (Dwarf for survivability, Draenei for threat)
- **Horde:** Blood Elf (only option, excellent utility)

## Closing Notes

This research document covers the foundational elements of TBC Paladin tanking beyond gear selection. Key takeaways:

1. **Consumables:** Always flask for raids (Fortification for bosses, Blinding Light for AOE). Stack Ironshield Potion + Elixir of Major Defense for physical damage. Dark Runes are mandatory for mana management.

2. **Enchantments:** Prioritize defense cap first, then stamina. Mongoose is BiS weapon enchant for versatility. Threat enchant on gloves is mandatory.

3. **Gems:** Eternal Earthstorm Diamond is non-negotiable. Maintain 2 more blue than red gems. Solid Star of Elune for blue sockets, Glowing Nightseye for red (threat builds).

4. **Professions:** Engineering is king for utility (Tankatronic Goggles, Rocket Launcher, Sappers). Jewelcrafting or Enchanting for pure stats. Drop gathering professions at 70.

5. **Macros:** Righteous Defense mouseover macro is essential. Divine Shield + cancel macro saves lives. Use WeakAuras over complex macros for ability tracking.

6. **Addons:** PallyPower, Omen, ThreatPlates, and WeakAuras are mandatory. DBM/BigWigs for raid mechanics.

7. **Races:** Dwarf for survivability (Stoneform), Draenei for threat (Heroic Presence), Blood Elf for Horde (Arcane Torrent).

This document serves as a comprehensive reference for optimizing Paladin tank performance in TBC raiding environments. Adjust strategies based on specific encounter requirements and guild composition.

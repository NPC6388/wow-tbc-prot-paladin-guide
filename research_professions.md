# TBC Professions for Prot Paladin — Complete Research

**Date**: 2026-04-21
**Purpose**: Comprehensive breakdown of every TBC profession's value for a Protection Paladin tank, covering BoP items, unique perks, phase scaling, and recommended combinations.

**Sources**: Wowhead TBC, Warcraft Tavern, Icy Veins, Wowpedia, CavernOfTime TBC DB, WoWWiki Archive, Blizzard Forums, wow-professions.com, various community guides.

## Tier 1: Strong Combat Benefit

---

### ENGINEERING (Best Overall)

Engineering is the consensus #1 profession for Prot Paladin tanks across all phases. It provides in-combat tools no other profession can match.

#### Bombs & Sappers (The Killer Feature)

| Item | Damage | Effect | Requirement |
|---|---|---|---|
| Super Sapper Charge | 900–1,500 Fire AoE | Self-damage 675–1,125 | Goblin Eng (340) to craft |
| Goblin Sapper Charge | 450–750 Fire AoE | Self-damage 375–625 | Goblin Eng to craft |
| Adamantite Grenade | 450–750 Fire | 3s stun, 3yd radius | Eng (325) |
| Fel Iron Bomb | 330–770 Fire | 3s stun, 5yd radius | Eng (300) |
| Dense Dynamite | ~340–460 Fire | Classic-era, cheap | Eng (250) |

**Why this matters for Prot Paladin specifically:**
- Consecration ticks once per second for 8 seconds — AoE threat ramps slowly
- A bomb/sapper on pull instantly puts threat on every mob, buying time for Consec to take hold
- Without bombs, loose mobs run to healers during the first 2–3 seconds
- Sapper self-damage triggers Spiritual Attunement (10% of healing received = mana), making sappers partially mana-neutral
- Super Sapper + Goblin Sapper share a 5-minute cooldown family — carry both, alternate

- Goblin Rocket Launcher is outstanding: +45 stam is huge for P1, the on-use is a **ranged pull + stun** solving Prot Paladin's biggest weakness (no ranged pull besides AS on 30s CD)
- +45 Stamina trinkets are nearly impossible to find in Phase 1 from other sources

**Goblin wins for tanking:**
1. Goblin Rocket Launcher >> Gnomish Poultryizer (ranged pull + stun vs backfire-prone CC)
2. Self-craft Super Sapper Charges and Goblin Sapper Charges (constant consumption, self-sufficiency saves gold)
3. Dimensional Ripper: Area 52 is more useful (Netherstorm = Tempest Keep raids)

### LEATHERWORKING (Drums)

The main value is **Drums of Battle** — a party-wide buff only usable by Leatherworkers.

#### Drums

| Drum | Effect | Duration | Cooldown |
|---|---|---|---|
| Drums of Battle | +80 Haste Rating (melee, ranged, spell) to party | 30s | 2min shared |
| Drums of War | +60 AP, +30 Spell Damage to party | 30s | 2min shared |
| Drums of Restoration | 600 HP + 600 Mana to party over 15s | 15s | 2min shared |
| Drums of Speed | +15% movement speed to party | 30s | 2min shared |

**Drum Rotation (Original TBC / Pre-Nerf):**
Raids stacked 5 LWs per group, rotating drums every 30s for permanent 100% uptime on +80 haste. This was so powerful it felt mandatory for every raider to be LW.

**Tinnitus Nerf (TBC Classic):**
Blizzard added a 2-minute **Tinnitus** debuff after using drums, preventing reuse. Result:
- Only 1 LW per party is needed (30s buff / 2min CD = **25% uptime**)
- Still strong, but no longer requires the entire raid to be LW
- Tinnitus was originally added in the patch after Sunwell in retail TBC — TBC Classic retroactively applied it from launch

**Greater Drums (TBC Classic addition):**
Upgraded versions with the same effect that apply to the whole raid group. Require higher LW skill.

#### Other LW Items

- **Nethercleft Leg Armor**: +40 Stam, +12 Agi — BoE, anyone can buy/use, no LW advantage
- **Vindicator's Armor Kit**: +8 Defense Rating to chest — BoE
- All crafted armor is leather/mail — useless for plate Paladins
- LW specializations (Dragonscale, Elemental, Tribal) produce leather/mail sets irrelevant to Paladins

#### Verdict

Strong if your raid needs a drummer in the tank group. The +80 haste for 30s is significant (more auto-attacks = more seal procs = more threat + faster SoC stacking). But it's a party buff, not a personal perk — if someone else in your group already has LW, the value drops to zero.

### ENCHANTING (Ring Enchants)

The unique perk is **self-only ring enchants** on both rings.

#### Ring Enchant Options

| Enchant | Bonus (per ring) | Total (2 rings) | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Enchant Ring - Spellpower | +12 Spell Damage | **+24 Spell Damage** | Threat |
| Enchant Ring - Stats | +4 All Stats | **+8 All Stats** | Balance (Stam+Int+etc) |
| Enchant Ring - Healing Power | +20 Healing / +7 SP | +14 Spell Damage total | Healing (worse for Prot) |
| Enchant Ring - Striking | +2 Weapon Damage | +4 Weapon Damage | Melee DPS (useless for Prot) |

**For Prot Paladin:** +24 Spell Damage is the clear pick. Permanent, always-on, no cooldown, no consumable cost, no effort. At 556 SP, +24 SP is ~4.3% more spell power scaling every ability.

**Formula Sources:**
- Enchant Ring - Spellpower: Sold by Alurmi (Keepers of Time, Revered)
- Enchant Ring - Stats: Sold by Nakodu (Lower City, Revered)
- Materials: Large Prismatic Shards, Void Crystals, Nexus Crystals, Greater/Lesser Planar Essences

**Other Enchanting perks:**
- Disenchanting raid drops for shards/crystals (gold + guild enchanting mats)
- Profitable profession (enchanting services, selling mats)

#### Verdict

Reliable, permanent combat boost. Less flashy than Engineering but always-on. Pairs well as a second profession alongside Engineering.

### JEWELCRAFTING (BoP Gems + Trinkets)

#### JC-Only BoP Gems (Unique-Equipped)

| Gem | Color | Stat | Best Normal Gem | Advantage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Falling Star | Blue | +18 Stamina | Solid Star of Elune (+12 Stam) | **+6 Stam** |
| Don Julio's Heart | Red | +14 Spell Damage | Runed Living Ruby (+9 SP) | **+5 SP** |

- Unique-equipped: only 1 of each can be socketed
- BoP and causes the item to become soulbound when socketed
- Designs from reputation vendors: Falling Star (Lower City Revered, JC 365), Don Julio's Heart (Consortium Revered, JC 360)
- Modest advantage over normal gems but permanent and free

#### JC-Only BoP Trinkets (Figurines)

| Trinket | Phase | Passive Stats | On-Use Effect | iLvl |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Figurine - Dawnstone Crab | P1 | +32 Defense Rating | +126 Dodge Rating for 20s | 115 |
| Figurine - Living Ruby Serpent | P1 | +2 Int, +3 Stam | +150 Spell Power for 20s | 115 |
| Figurine - Talasite Owl | P1 | (mana trinket) | Restores 900 mana over 12s | 115 |
| Figurine - Nightseye Panther | P1 | (melee trinket) | +320 AP for 12s | 115 |
| Figurine - Crimson Serpent | P5 | +33 Int, +49 Stam | +151 Spell Power for 20s | 125 |

**Key trinkets for Prot Paladin:**

- **Dawnstone Crab** (P1): Excellent. +32 passive defense helps reach crit immunity cap, +126 dodge on-use is a strong survival cooldown. Near-BiS defensive trinket in Phase 1. Design from Honor Hold/Thrallmar (Revered), JC 375.
- **Living Ruby Serpent** (P1): Threat trinket. +150 SP for 20s is a huge burst window for burn phases/openers. JC 370.
- **Crimson Serpent** (P5 upgrade): Massive base stats (+49 Stam, +33 Int) plus +151 SP on-use. Competes with raid trinkets. Design from Shattered Sun Offensive rep. JC 375.

#### Other JC Value

- One of the best gold-making professions (gem cuts are in constant demand from raiders re-gemming)
- Prospecting ore into gems feeds the gem market

#### Phase Scaling

| Phase | Value |
|---|---|
| P1 | HIGH — Dawnstone Crab is near-BiS, Living Ruby Serpent for threat, gem advantage |
| P2–P4 | Moderate — gem advantage persists, trinkets get outclassed by raid drops |
| P5 | HIGH again — Crimson Serpent (+49 Stam, +151 SP on-use) is excellent |

#### Verdict

Strong in Phase 1 (Dawnstone Crab is near-BiS defensive trinket), modest gem advantage throughout, excellent P5 upgrade with Crimson Serpent. Good gold income.

### BLACKSMITHING (BoP Weapons + Armor)

BS offers BoP crafted weapons and armor through specializations, but **Prot Paladins want spell power weapons**, and BS weapons are physical DPS items.

#### Armorsmith BoP Chain (Plate Chest)

| Stage | Item | iLvl | Key Stats |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Breastplate of Kings | — | Pre-requisite |
| 2 | Bulwark of Kings | — | Pre-requisite |
| 3 | Bulwark of the Ancient Kings | 146 | 1,825 Armor, +56 Stam, +40 Str, +41 Crit Rating, +25 Hit Rating, 1 Blue + 2 Yellow sockets (+4 Crit bonus). **Use: +1,501 HP / +151 Str for 15s** |

This is a physical tank chestpiece — great stamina and an HP cooldown, but **no spell power, defense, or dodge**. It's a "survive through raw HP" piece rather than a threat piece. The on-use +1,501 HP is a solid emergency cooldown. Situationally useful but competes with tier sets that have SP and tankier stats.

**Mail variant:** Nether Chain Shirt → Twisting Nether Chain Shirt → Embrace of the Twisting Nether (Armorsmith mail chain — irrelevant to plate Paladins)

The Dragonstrike haste proc is theoretically interesting (more swings = more seal procs) but the 2.70 speed and zero SP make it far worse than a fast SP weapon for sustained threat.

#### Notable BoE Crafted Plate (Anyone Can Equip)

| Item | Slot | Key Stats | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Felsteel Helm / Breastplate / Leggings | Head/Chest/Legs | Entry-level tanking plate (defense, stam) | BS trainer recipes |

**Important:** The BoE items don't require being a Blacksmith to wear — you can buy them from other players. Only the specialization chains (Bulwark, Blazefury, etc.) and Boots of the Protector are BoP.

#### Verdict

Mediocre for Prot Paladin. Armorsmith gives one decent survivability chest, but no spell power items. The specialization weapons are all physical — completely wrong for a spell power tank. Only consider BS if you also play Ret off-spec where weapons like Blazefury or Dragonstrike are relevant.

### ALCHEMY (Trinket + Potion Bonus)

#### Alchemist's Stone (Phase 1)

- **Item Level**: 90
- **Stats**: +15 Str, +15 Agi, +15 Stam, +15 Int, +15 Spi
- **Equip**: Increases the effect of healing and mana potions by 40%
- **Requires**: Alchemy 350, BoP

The +40% potion bonus is strong for mana-hungry Prot Paladins:
- Super Mana Potion: 1,800–3,000 → **2,520–4,200 mana** (+720–1,200 extra)
- Healing pots similarly boosted for emergency survival
- The +15 all stats is weak but provides some of everything

#### Upgraded Stones (Patch 2.4 / Phase 5, Shattered Sun Offensive)

| Stone | Key Stat | Potion Bonus | iLvl | Crafting |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Guardian's Alchemist Stone | +6 Dodge | +40% potions | 125 | 1x Alchemist's Stone + 6x Primal Air + 2x Nether Vortex |
| Sorcerer's Alchemist Stone | +63 Spell Damage | +40% potions | 125 | Similar materials |
| Redeemer's Alchemist Stone | +120 Healing / +41 SP | +40% potions | 125 | Similar materials |
| Assassin's Alchemist Stone | (melee DPS) | +40% potions | 125 | Similar materials |

**Sorcerer's Stone** (+63 SP + 40% pots) is the best option for a threat-focused Prot Paladin, but by Phase 5 you likely have better raid trinkets.

**Guardian's Stone** (+6 Dodge + 40% pots) is underwhelming — +6 Dodge is negligible compared to Figurine - Dawnstone Crab's +32 Defense.

#### Alchemy Specializations

| Spec | Perk | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Transmute Master | Chance for extra procs on transmutes | Gold-making (Primal Might, etc.) |
| Elixir Master | Chance for extra procs on flasks/elixirs | Saving gold on consumables |
| Potion Master | Chance for extra procs on potions | Personal savings / guild supply |

#### Mad Alchemist's Potion

- Restores 1,650–2,750 health AND mana
- Also grants a random elixir effect for 2 hours
- Usable by anyone, NOT Alchemy-only
- Shares potion cooldown

#### Cauldrons

- Greater Cauldrons create flasks for the whole raid from a single crafting
- Require Alchemy to create, but anyone can loot from them
- Primarily a guild convenience / cost-saving tool

#### Verdict

The +40% mana potion bonus is genuinely good for Prot Paladin mana sustain. The trinkets are decent early but get outclassed. Main long-term value is economic (cheaper consumables, transmute gold). Lower tier than Engineering/Enchanting for combat power.

## Tier 3: Minimal / No Combat Benefit

---

### TAILORING

**Almost zero value for Prot Paladin.** All crafted armor is cloth.

- **Spellthread leg enchants** (Runic Spellthread: +35 SP / +20 Stam) are BoE — anyone can buy and apply them without Tailoring
- **Netherweave Net** is usable by anyone, not Tailor-only
- **Cloth cooldowns** (Spellcloth, Shadowcloth, Primal Mooncloth) are pure gold-making

**Verdict:** No combat benefit. Pure gold-making profession. Do not take on your Prot Paladin.

---

### MINING

**No passive combat bonus in TBC.** The +Stamina "Toughness" passive was added in WotLK.

- Feeds Engineering, Jewelcrafting, and Blacksmithing
- Good gold from Adamantite/Khorium ore + Mote of Fire/Earth drops
- ~200–500g/hr depending on routes and competition
- Felsteel Bar and Hardened Adamantite Bar are smelting-only products (can't be obtained otherwise)

**Verdict:** Pure economic value. Best gathering prof if you must take one (feeds Engineering). Put on an alt if possible.

---

### HERBALISM

**No passive combat bonus in TBC.** Lifeblood heal was added in WotLK.

- Arguably best raw gold-making gathering profession
- Fel Lotus (~4% bonus drop from any Outland node) always premium-priced
- Mana Thistle (375 skill, flask ingredient) commands high prices
- Bonus Mote of Life / Mote of Mana drops from nodes
- Feeds Alchemy

**Verdict:** Best gold per hour of any gathering profession, but zero combat power. Put on an alt (ideally a Druid for instant flight-form herb picks).

---

### SKINNING

**No passive combat bonus in TBC.** +Crit "Master of Anatomy" was added in WotLK.

- Weakest gold of the three gathering professions
- Thick Clefthoof Leather has niche value (Drums of Battle material)
- Requires killing mobs first, slower than herb/ore node flying routes
- Feeds Leatherworking

**Verdict:** Worst gathering profession for Prot Paladin. Only take if paired with LW on an alt for self-supply.

## Recommended Profession Combinations

| Combo | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|---|
| **Engineering + Enchanting** | Best overall. AoE bombs/sappers for threat + permanent +24 SP from rings. Covers both threat and survivability tools. | Two crafting profs = expensive to level, need alt for gathering |
| **Engineering + Jewelcrafting** | Strong Phase 1. Goblin Rocket Launcher + Dawnstone Crab = two great trinkets from professions alone. JC makes gold and provides gem advantage. | Enchanting ring bonus is slightly higher raw combat value than JC gems |
| **Engineering + Leatherworking** | If raid needs you to drum. Bombs for AoE + Drums of Battle (+80 haste) for your party. | Drums only 25% uptime post-Tinnitus; useless if another LW in group |
| **Engineering + Alchemy** | Budget option. +40% mana pots helps sustain, cheaper consumables long-term. Transmute Master for gold. | Alchemist Stone trinket is weak; main value is economic not combat |
| **Jewelcrafting + Enchanting** | If you refuse Engineering. +24 SP rings + BoP gems + BoP trinkets. Solid passive stat gains. | No AoE threat tools, no ranged pull gadgets, no repair bots |

**Engineering appears in every top combination** because nothing else provides in-combat AoE threat tools and ranged pulling gadgets. The second slot depends on raid needs and gold situation.
